TEDxYouth@St.GeorgesTech

During my junior year of highschool we were tasked with making a website for a TEDxYouth presentation that would inform people as to where the event would take place, who will be participating and speaking, and what the event was.

Utilizing HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap we were able to make this website. In this website your able to get an overview as to what TEDxYouth is and who would be speaking at the event. There is also a sign up page in case the user wants to be a speaker at the event.

Senior Project

My Senior Project was a game called Luckie's Journey. It was built with the Godot Engine.

In the game you play as Luckie the lumberjack and your goal to kill all 20 loggers. However, your being chased by a demon, named U.Le. If U.Le touches one of the loggers, then it's game over, but if Luckie defeats them all he wins! The controls for the game are WASD to move and press and hold Space to swing Luckie's hatchet.

Higher or Lower Card Game

In my sophmore year of highschool, we were tasked with making two card games with our knowledge of Java.

Utilizing Java the game takes a classic card game and turns it into an application. In this game, the name of a card will display and you have to guess if the next card will have a higher or lower value. If you get it correct, then you will get a point. Once you get one wrong, you can either bank all the points you earned or risk them all to double the current amount you have. The game is over once you have banked 15 points.
